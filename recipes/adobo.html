Adobo

Adobo from another galaxy

When people eat adobo from this recipe they fucking die.

Its so good you turn into an alien.

Ingrediants

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup low sodium soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Head the vegetable oil
  2. Cook the chicken until its brown
  3. Add in the onion, garlic, and soy sauce
  4. Cook for 40 minutes
