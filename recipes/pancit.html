Pancit

Sexy Pancit

Eating this dish is like having a thousand tentacles pleasure you from inside

Every strand of noodle gives you sensory overload.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Dried rice noodles package
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small head cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 4 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 2 lemons - cut into wedges, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soften the rice noodles
  2. Cook the onions and garlic in oil, then add the remaining ingrediants
  3. Cook until the cabbage is soft, then stir in the softened noodles
  4. Garnish with lemon wedges
